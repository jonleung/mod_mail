ModMail

Because we could all use a second chance.

What is it?

ModMail lets you send emails and edit the message later. Magic!

Anyone with a Gmail account can sign up and try it now.

Sign up now

How to use

  1. Compose an email to send@modmail.cc.
  2. List your recepients on separate lines at the beginning of the body.
  3. Write your message.
  4. Send!

You'll receive an email that you can use to edit the email that you just sent. Go crazy.
